AADINATH EDUCATION TRUST

Rajkot , Gujarat

Unique Id : GJ/2020/0256487

Mobile Number : 9426906883

Sector Working In : Education

Lack of financial resources should not deprive any Jain Student from achieving his full potential in attaining his educational objectives. To this end, we strive to provide financial assistance to academically proficient but economically weak Jain students to pursue their further education whether by way of outright grant or refundable loan (without interest), depending upon the individual circumstances. The Trust Goal is to carry them along so that they bring glory & respect for Jain Community.

ANDHJAN VIVIDHLAXI TALIM KENDRA

Jamnagar , Gujarat

Unique Id : GJ/2016/0111100

Mobile Number : 94279437210

Sector Working In : Differently Abled

Andhjan Vividhlaxi Talim Kendra came into existence on 9th February 1969 in Jamnagar. To start with, the institution commenced functioning in a rented building with the enrolment of 3 blind students.
